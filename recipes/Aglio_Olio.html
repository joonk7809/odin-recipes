Aglio Olio Oil Pasta

Description:

The photo above contains a complted aglio olio in its full glory with plating done impeccably. The sweet scent of half-caramelized garlic, the perfect middle ground between oily and saucy, this pasta is the go to for any situation.

Ingredients:

  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Pasta
  • Peperoncino pepper

    • Steps:

    1. Boil pasta noodles for 6 minutes.
    2. Pour olive oil onto the pan and heat the pan up to a certain degree.
    3. Once the oil is hot, put the garlic in. Make sure it is at a temperature where the garlic will not turn brown.
    4. Put some whole peperoncino peppers into the pan. 2-3 is recommended for mild-medium spiciness.
    5. Once garlic + peperoncino oil is made, it should be about 5-6 minutes in. Take your noodles out of the water, sift it, and put it in the pan.
    6. As you swivel the pan around for the oil and pasta to stick together, pour some pasta water into the pan for emulsion.

    There you have it! your amazing aglio olio complted. Enjoy your dish!
