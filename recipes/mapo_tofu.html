Mapo Tofu Recipe:

Step 1:prepare ingredients

  • Cut up some tofu
  • Cut up some scallions
  • Chop up some chinese chilies

    • Step 2: Fire up!

  • Start frying chillies with oil on your wok
  • Add the meat to the pan and make some magic happen!
  • Add the scallions and onions on the pan, and fry until all the sauce is merged with the ingredients

    • Step 3: Prepare to serve!

  • Put rice in the pan and fry until all the sauces and ingredients become one!
  • Now it is ready to serve to your guests!
